Andrew L. Wang is a journalist based in Los Angeles. He has written for the Associated Press, the Los Angeles Times, the Chicago Tribune and Crain's Chicago Business.

He currently writes about small business at NerdWallet, a personal-finance site.

When he's not writing he enjoys photography and exploring the outdoors.

Andrew has a bachelor's degree in mechanical engineering from Duke University, a master's degree in journalism from Northwestern University and an MBA, from Northwestern's Kellogg School of Management.

Find him on Twitter at @andrew_L_wang, Instagram at @andrewwang13 and LinkedIn.
